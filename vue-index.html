BDO Crystalz

Black Desert Online Crystal Reference


Need to know if there is an AP crystal for your offhand weapon? Now you can find out!

This is a reference for Black Desert Online items called crystals. Crystals are used to slot into specific gear pieces in order to give you some added effects. With this site, you can easily find which crystals suit your specific needs by filtering by the crystal's grade, the effects it grants, which slot it goes in, and how often it is likely to break when you die.
